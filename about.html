Eco-friendly Landscaping services in Queens, NY

Singh Landscaping & Lawn Sprinkler, Corp. has been serving the New York metropolitan area for over 20 years. Founded by Ricky Singh, our company is built on a foundation of quality, reliability, and environmental responsibility.

We are a fully licensed and insured landscaping and general contractor company. Our team consists of experienced professionals who are dedicated to transforming outdoor spaces into beautiful, functional environments that our clients are proud of.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide outstanding landscaping and asphalt painting services that exceed our clients’ expectations while maintaining environmental sustainability. We believe that beautiful outdoor spaces contribute to the well-being of individuals and communities alike.

Our Experience

With more than two decades of experience in the landscaping industry, we have built a strong reputation for excellence in Queens, New York. We are established as a landscape and general contractor where we serve outstanding clients such as the NYC School Construction Authority, City of New York Park and Recreation, New York State of Opportunity, New York City Transit, and the NYC Department of Design and Construction.
