Who We Are

Tom & Jerry Builders —
Built on Trust & Quality

Tom and Jerry Builders is a fully licensed and insured asphalt painting and construction company with over 15 years of experience serving Queens, Long Island, and the greater New York metropolitan area.

We specialize in asphalt paving, playground marking, retaining walls, lawn care, and all forms of outdoor construction. Our commitment is simple: show up on time, do the work right, and leave your property better than we found it.

Our team uses only premium materials — including Plexipave and Sport Master asphalt products with over 300 color and logo options — ensuring every project is built to last.
